Rice Disease Clinic

Identify symptoms and protect your paddy harvest

Fungal Disease

Rice Blast

Caused by Magnaporthe oryzae, it's one of the most destructive diseases.

Symptoms:
  • Spindle-shaped spots with gray centers.
  • Brownish borders on leaves.
  • "Neck rot" causing heads to fall over.
AI Feature:

Detected via leaf spot pattern recognition and humidity sensor data.

Bacterial

Bacterial Leaf Blight

Spreads quickly through irrigation water and during heavy rains.

Symptoms:
  • Yellowish streaks starting from leaf tips.
  • Milky dew drops on leaves in early morning.
  • Leaves dry up and look "burnt".
AI Feature:

Real-time alerts triggered by monsoon flood patterns and temperature spikes.

Soil-borne

Sheath Blight

Common in high-density planting areas with high nitrogen use.

Symptoms:
  • Oval or ellipsoid greenish-gray lesions.
  • Found near the water line on leaf sheaths.
  • Irregular "snake-skin" patterns.
AI Feature:

Analyzes soil nitrogen levels to predict outbreak probability.
